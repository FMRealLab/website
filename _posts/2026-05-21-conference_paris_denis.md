Fany was invited to present at the conference ["L’évolution des mécanismes du développement"](https://www.college-de-france.fr/fr/agenda/colloque/evolution-des-mecanismes-du-developpement), organized by evolutionary biologist Denis Duboule.

The symposium focused on how developmental processes create biological variation, the basis of evolution. While natural selection determines which traits persist, the meeting addressed a more fundamental question: how new forms arise in the first place.
