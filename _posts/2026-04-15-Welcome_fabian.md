We’re thrilled to welcome Fabian Lim to the Real Lab as a postdoctoral researcher.

At the Real Lab, Fabian will be exploring the fascinating biology of the Iberian mole, comparing the 3D genomic architecture and enhancers between moles and mice to uncover mechanisms of evolutionary adaptation behind specialized traits.
