During the 1st General Meeting of the Spanish EvoDevOmics Network in Barcelona, Fany, Nacho and Rubén took part in an outreach activity aimed at bringing developmental and evolutionary biology closer to a broad audience.

They showcased beautiful skeletal stainings of mouse embryos, allowing attendees to observe fine anatomical details and appreciate how skeletal structures emerge during development. In parallel, they presented a series of slides highlighting the fascinating traits of moles: what these unique animals are like, their remarkable adaptations, and how researchers find and study them in the wild.
